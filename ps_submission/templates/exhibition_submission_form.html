SUBMIT ARCHIVE

If you would like to submit an activity for our archive please fill out our form below. Critera for submissions is that the activity has been held in Denmark or with artists whom resides in Denmark, that you have the rights to publish the activity, and that the activity is well-documented*. When you submit the activity, you accept the possibility of being featured on the index as a highlight chosen by our board of artist-moderators, as well as on our social media account.

* Well-documented means:
- A minimum of 5 images and a maximum of 20 images.
- Images should have a minimum of 150 dpi and 2000px in width, and a maximum of 3 mb per image.
- Allowed image file extensions are: png, jpg, jpeg, webp.
- Remember to credit artist(s) and photographer(s) in captions.

Please note, we do not have the capacity to correct errors, typos, rearrange images etc. after your submission has been published. If you find an error in your post, please re-submit, and we will delete the previous post.
